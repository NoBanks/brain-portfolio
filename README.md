# BRAIN

**Personal AI scientific research partner. 1,925-paper corpus, evidence-grading, blind-spots footer, drag-and-drop PDF onboarding. Built for a working biotech CEO, not for a demo.**

---

## What it is

BRAIN is a sovereign personal research AI built for Kate (CEO of DNKO, glioblastoma biotech). Internal tool, not a public-facing product. Lives in daily use against a 1,925-paper knowledge base: 237 Needham corpus papers, 671 citation-network papers, 536 broader GBM papers, 481 cutting-edge 2024-2026 papers (459 from 2026 alone). 38 papers have full section-by-section analysis. 2,694 searchable passages.

This repo is the public marketing surface that documents the architecture and the use case. The product itself stays private because the corpus and the workflows are personal to Kate's research.

## Stack

TypeScript, Node, local MLX inference. Telegram command surface (/challenge for evidence-grading, drag-and-drop PDF onboarding). Vector store + retrieval over the cleaned 1,925-paper corpus. Blind-spots footer surfaces gaps in the corpus when relevant.

---

## Source private by design

BRAIN is internal to DNKO. The source and the corpus are not public. This repo documents the architecture publicly so the build pattern is reusable; the specific knowledge base and workflows stay with Kate.
